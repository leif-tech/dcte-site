About DCTE

Davao Computer Trade-in Express (DCTE) is the first computer store in the Philippines to offer a dedicated PC parts trade-in program. We make upgrading your setup easy and affordable - bring in your old components, get them assessed, and walk out with better hardware at a fraction of the price.

Whether you're a student building your first PC, a gamer chasing higher frames, or a professional needing a workstation upgrade, DCTE has the parts, expertise, and the best deals in Mindanao.
